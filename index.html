What does your browser actually tell the sites you visit?
Run a live scan and see the exact data points that build a fingerprint of your device, then get a plain score for how exposed you are.
About this tool

TraceLine is open source and runs entirely in your browser. It pulls together the same signals real tracking scripts use, canvas and audio reads, GPU info, fonts, hardware specs, and shows you the full picture in one place.

Before you run it

Clicking the button below lets this page read your browser APIs the same way a tracking script would. That means hardware details, network info, and anything else a normal site could pull without asking.

Nothing here touches a server. Everything is read locally and gone the moment you close the tab.
